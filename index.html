Announcing the MIC Pre-Accelerator Program!


A group of Microsoft Innovation Centers (MIC's) are linked by a unique startup training program, connecting entrepreneurs to resources and support they need in validating and building their ideas into solutions. Over the 12 week intensive program, the selected teams are taken through specialized training and have access and support from the international community. The program culminates in the MIC DEMO WEEK in April, where a global audience will witness the product demonstrations and startups will receive feedback from experts in each of the partner countries.

Guidelines


The MIC Pre-Accelerator Program is founder-friendly, focuses on individual development, and supports big, disrupt-the-industry visions


  • The Benefits

    NO equity taken in your company! · A top-notch curriculum of highly practical workshops and training sessions · Access to the collaborative working space in the MIC office · Mentoring from a vetted network of local, national and international entrepreneurs and experts · Exposure to qualified investors · A demo week celebration · And more awesome surprises...

  • Your Team

    We seek teams of 2 to 4 founders with complementary skills (business AND technical, design a plus). Your team should be prepared to devote 40 hours+ weekly during the length of the program. Founding team members must speak English. We place a great deal of importance on the team, how solid they are and their ability to be coached and to take feedback.

  • Your Business

    The concept should be an innovative web or mobile technology solution that addresses a clear market need – both in your country and the region, at a minimum. Your business should still be in the idea phase, you may be doing research and speaking with customers while defining your value proposition. The company should still be pre-revenue. Having some form of an intellectual property (patents, trademarks) is a plus but not a requirement.
